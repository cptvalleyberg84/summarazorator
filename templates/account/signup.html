Community Guidelines

By creating an account, you agree to follow these community guidelines:

  1. Respect all community members regardless of their background, identity, or opinions.
  2. Post content in appropriate sections:
    • Positive comments and feedback must be posted in the positive section only
    • Negative comments and feedback must be posted in the negative section only
    • Deliberately posting in wrong sections may result in account suspension or permanent ban
  3. No hate speech, harassment, or bullying of any kind will be tolerated.
  4. Keep discussions constructive and relevant to the topic.
  5. Do not share personal or sensitive information about others.
  6. No spam, advertising, or self-promotion without permission.
  7. Respect intellectual property rights and give credit when using others' content.
  8. Report inappropriate content or behavior to moderators.

Note: Violation of these guidelines, especially regarding correct section posting, may result in immediate account suspension or permanent ban at moderators' discretion.
